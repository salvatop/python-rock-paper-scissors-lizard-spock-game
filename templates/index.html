Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock

Rock-paper-scissors-lizard-Spock is a variant of Rock-paper-scissors that allows five choices.
Each choice wins against two other choices, loses against two other choices and ties against itself.
The Wikipedia entry gives the complete description of the details of the game.
